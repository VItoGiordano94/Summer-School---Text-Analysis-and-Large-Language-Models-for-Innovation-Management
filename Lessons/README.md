# Lessons

This folder contains the PDF versions of the slides presented during the lessons of the **Summer School on Text Analysis and Large Language Models for Innovation Management**, organised by the University of Pisa.

The materials are organised according to the Summer School programme and cover the main conceptual, methodological, and practical topics addressed during the course.

## Use of the Materials

These slides are intended primarily for participants in the Summer School and may be used for educational and research purposes.

Participants are encouraged to use the materials as a reference during the practical sessions and after the completion of the Summer School. When using or referring to specific content, please acknowledge the instructor and the Summer School.
