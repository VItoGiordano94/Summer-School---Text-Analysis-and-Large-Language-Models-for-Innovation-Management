# Keynote Speakers

This folder contains the materials shared by the **keynote speakers** invited to the **Summer School on Text Analysis and Large Language Models for Innovation Management**, organised by the University of Pisa.

The keynote sessions complement the main lessons and practical activities of the Summer School by providing participants with expert perspectives on emerging developments, methodological challenges, and academic perspective on text analysis, Natural Language Processing, and Large Language Models.

## Use of the Materials

The materials are intended primarily for participants in the Summer School and may be used for educational and research purposes.

When referring to or reusing specific content, participants should appropriately acknowledge the keynote speaker, the presentation title, and the **Summer School on Text Analysis and Large Language Models for Innovation Management**.
